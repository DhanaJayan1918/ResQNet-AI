# Wildfire Evacuation and Containment (SOP-WF-03)

## 1. Wildfire Evacuation Corridors
Identify safe escape routes away from wind direction. Coordinate with police to enforce one-way outbound lanes on narrow forest corridors. Set up check-points at evacuation exits.

## 2. Air Quality and Public Safety
Issue high-level smoke alerts. Distribute N95 respirators to volunteers and citizens. Instruct residents within a 5-mile radius to close all windows and stay indoors.

## 3. Firefighting Asset Allocation
Prioritize aerial water drops on windward fronts to block fire expansion toward residential communities.
