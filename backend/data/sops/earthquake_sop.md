# Earthquake Structural Collapse Response (SOP-EQ-02)

## 1. Search and Rescue in Rubble
Use heavy rescue equipment, sonar detectors, and canine teams. Clear access routes for ambulances immediately. Triage injuries on-site: Red tag (immediate), Yellow tag (delayed), Green tag (minor).

## 2. Hospital Load Management
Divert non-critical victims to peripheral medical centers. Keep tertiary hospitals clear for surgeries. Check structural integrity of hospital buildings before admitting emergency cases inside.

## 3. Emergency Shelter Setup
Establish open-air tent shelters in parks or stadiums. Avoid setting up indoor shelters near tall structures subject to collapse during aftershocks.
