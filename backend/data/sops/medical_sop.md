# Medical Triage and Epidemic Containment (SOP-MD-04)

## 1. Mass Casualty Triage Protocols
Establish on-scene medical triage areas. Triage levels: Category 1 (immediate resuscitation), Category 2 (major trauma / stabilization), Category 3 (walking wounded).

## 2. Contagion and Waterborne Disease Control
Isolate patients showing symptoms of cholera, gastroenteritis, or typhoid. Treat relief camp water sources with chlorine. Ensure clean sanitary facilities are separated from dining zones.
