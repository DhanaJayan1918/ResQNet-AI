# Flood Emergency Response Protocol (SOP-FL-01)

## 1. Life Safety and Active Water Rescue
Immediately deploy rescue boats to priority points. Prioritize families trapped on roofs, vulnerable children, and the elderly. All field officers must wear high-buoyancy life jackets.

## 2. Relief Camp and Shelter Operations
Direct displaced persons to high-ground schools and community shelters. Setup clean water filtration stations and distribute food rations. Coordinate medical checkups for waterborne disease prevention.

## 3. Power Grid and Electrical Isolation
Isolate sub-stations in inundated regions. Coordinate with utility companies to shut down power grids where water levels exceed 3 feet to prevent electrocution hazards.
